Why this tool exists

Writing about yourself professionally is often harder than writing about your work. Many people have excellent internships, full-time roles, academic achievements, project experience, certifications, and technical skills, but still struggle to present those strengths clearly on LinkedIn. This website exists to make that process faster and easier. The goal is not to replace your voice, but to help you express it more clearly, confidently, and professionally.
